# Parego — App Overview

**Pair. Play. Go.** Simple over-the-board chess tournament management.

---

## What is Parego?

Parego helps you run and play in **real-life chess tournaments**—the kind where you sit across the board from your opponent. No complex setup, no admin software feel. Think of it like walking into a tournament hall and getting pairings on your phone.

---

## For Organizers

**Create a tournament in minutes.**

- Set a name, time, and location
- Choose your pairing style (arena, balanced strength, etc.)
- Share a link or QR code so players can join

**During the event:**

- Add players (search registered users or add guests)
- Start the tournament—pairings are generated automatically
- Mark players present when they arrive (or use GPS check-in)
- Results sync instantly; standings update in real time

**Venue check-in:**

- Players must be at the venue to join (GPS proximity, ~150 m)
- You can manually mark anyone present if needed

---

## For Players

**Find and join tournaments.**

- Browse tournaments near you on the home page
- Or use a join link or QR code shared by the organizer
- Express interest in upcoming events (optional)

**To join:**

- Be at the venue—the app checks you’re close enough
- Enter your strength (or use your profile if registered)
- Guests get a generated name; registered users use their profile

**During the tournament:**

- See your pairings and table number
- Submit your results
- View live standings

---

## Do I Need an Account?

- **Browse & express interest:** No account required
- **Join as guest:** No account—you get a temporary name
- **Join as registered user:** Sign up to track progress and use your rating
- **Create tournaments:** Account required

## Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Arena pairing** | Automatic round pairings and standings |
| **GPS check-in** | Ensures players are at the venue to join |
| **QR / share link** | Easy join flow for players |
| **Live standings** | Real-time results and leaderboard |
| **Nearby discovery** | Find tournaments by location |

---

## In Short

**Organizers:** Create → Share link → Start → Run pairings and results.

**Players:** Find or follow a link → Join at venue → Get pairings → Submit results.
